Community Setting

The MSU Computing Club is a relaxed learning environment where members can explore different areas of interest in the technology field and create a social environment students can interact with eachother to build professional relationships.

Fun Projects

Over the course of one semester a member can make smaller simple projects such as pong in pyhton or a simple personal website. Other times we create larger apps such as a Twitter clone using Pyhton, React natice dev for a moible app, and more!

Big Giveaways

At the end of each semster the club has its Coding Challenges where memeber can come compete for prizes such as custom laptops, iPads, airpods, phones and more! We do this as a thank you for coming ech semster. Ever year we give away about $14,000 worth pf prizes.
